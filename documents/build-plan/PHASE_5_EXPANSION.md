# Phase 5 – Expansion

## Purpose

Phase 5 focuses on improving platform maturity, automation, visibility, and operational efficiency.

This phase introduces enhancements that improve long-term maintainability and scalability.

---

# Objectives

The objectives of this phase are:

* Expand operational tooling
* Improve automation
* Enhance visibility
* Strengthen security controls
* Improve service management

---

# Operational Benefits

* Increased visibility
